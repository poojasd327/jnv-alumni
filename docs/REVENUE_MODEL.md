# Revenue Model — JNV Alumni Network Platform

## Market Context

- 661 JNV schools across India
- ~60 students per batch, ~38 batches (1986-2024)
- **~15 lakh total alumni** (1.5 million)
- Active on social media: 5-8 lakh
- Realistic platform users: 1-3 lakh
- Demographics: educated, employed, strong community ties, higher-than-average income

---

| Tier | Price | Features |

| **Business** | Rs 499/mo | All Premium + business listing boost, job posting boost, recruiter tools, bulk messaging |
